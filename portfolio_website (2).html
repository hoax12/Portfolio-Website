About Me

Hello, I'm Shreyash!

I'm an AI-focused master's student at USC with hands-on experience building LLM pipelines, deploying RAG agents, and solving applied ML problems in healthcare and developer tools. My research has enhanced diagnostic accuracy by 10-20% in medical imaging applications.

As a Research Assistant at USC and former AI Research Fellow at TIH-IoT IIT Bombay (awarded $7,100 Chanakya Fellowship), I've developed AI-based diagnostic tools that reduced diagnosis time from 30 minutes to under 5 minutes, with validation from 10+ dermatologists.

I'm passionate about leveraging deep learning and computer vision to solve real-world problems, particularly in healthcare applications. My work has been published in peer-reviewed conferences and recognized by industry stakeholders.
